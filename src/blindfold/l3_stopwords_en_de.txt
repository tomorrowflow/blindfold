# L3 stopwords (ADR-0023): a closed-class function-word list, EN+DE.
#
# Function words (articles, pronouns, prepositions, conjunctions, auxiliaries,
# common capitalized adverbs) are essentially never entity names, so suppressing
# them from L3 candidacy is a pure quality win against the candidate-span flood
# from agentic system prompts (issue #59). Suppression is token-granularity only
# and never affects L1/L2: a registered Term or entity-graph surface always wins
# regardless of whether its surface also appears here.
#
# One capitalized token per line (the shape L3's candidate regex matches:
# initial-cap + lowercase). Blank lines and lines starting with "#" are ignored.
